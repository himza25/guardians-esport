ABOUT GUARDIANS

Guardians Esport is a Moroccan competitive gaming organization with boundless ambitions to reach the highest levels of international esports. Based in Morocco, our elite squad represents the rising talent and strategic excellence of North African gaming.

Our flagship team, Guardians Esport(EMBR), consists of seven exceptional players who have dedicated thousands of hours perfecting their craft. From macro-aware specialists to explosive carry players, each member brings unique strengths that create an unstoppable competitive force.

Our Vision: To establish Morocco as a formidable presence in the global esports arena and prove that with dedication, strategy, and teamwork, geographical boundaries cannot limit competitive excellence.
